About me

J. Shamsul Bahri is a software engineer living and working in Singapore. Originally from Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, he is the husband to a beautiful wife and a father to an adorable daughter.

He believes that talking in third-person is necessary when writing an about page.

He does not believe in "eat, sleep code and repeat". While he is passionate about writing code and equally passionate about both eating and sleep, that is not all that he likes to do. He plays video games, read comics, brew and drink coffee, watch films, listen to music, collects and build mechanical keyboards and other random stuff.
